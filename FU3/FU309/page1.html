Q3: Where can you watch aurora borealis?

The aurora borealis can be observed primarily in high-latitude regions near the Arctic Circle, including northern Norway, Sweden, Finland, Iceland, Alaska, Yukon and Northwest Territories, and parts of Russia’s Arctic regions.
