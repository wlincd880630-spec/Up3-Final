What does the backpack include?
1. The backpack includes a tent, a lighter, and a compass.
2. Essential items are included in the backpack.
3. There are several items, including a sleeping bag and water.
1. Include (Verb)
Definition: To contain something as a part of something else, or to make something part of something else.
The package tour includes flights, hotels, and all meals. 这个跟团游套餐包含机票、酒店和所有餐食。
Does the price include tax and service charge? 这个价格包含税费和服务费吗？
2. Including (Preposition)
Definition: Used for saying that a person or thing is part of a particular group or amount.
Everyone has to work late tonight, including the manager. 今晚每个人都要加班，包括经理在内。
We visited several cities in Europe, including Paris and Rome. 我们参观了欧洲的几个城市，包括巴黎和罗马。
