A Relaxing Walking Tour in Chengdu
Chengdu is famous for its relaxed lifestyle, delicious food, and cute pandas. Follow this short walking tour to explore three amazing places in the city center!
Start at ① Kuanzhai Alley. This historic area has beautiful old buildings and teahouses. Try some local snacks like dandan noodles. You should sit in a teahouse and drink tea — it's the perfect way to feel real Chengdu life!
Next, walk about 15 minutes to ② Jinli Ancient Street. This lively street is full of red lanterns and shops. Don't miss the spicy skewers! If you like shopping, you should check out the souvenir stores.
Finally, walk to nearby ③ Wuhou Shrine. This peaceful temple has beautiful gardens and statues. You should spend some time walking slowly here to get away from the crowds. Don't forget to learn about Zhuge Liang from the Three Kingdoms!
